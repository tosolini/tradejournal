# Settings and Profile

## User Profile

Go to **Settings** in the sidebar to manage your profile.

### Timezone

The **Timezone** field is essential for the correct functioning of the market calendar.

- Select your geographic zone (e.g. `Europe/Rome` for Italy)
- The market calendar will automatically convert all times to your timezone
- If not configured, the browser's timezone is used (may differ)

**Common timezones:**

| Timezone | Description |
|---|---|
| `Europe/Rome` | Italy (CET/CEST) |
| `Europe/London` | United Kingdom (GMT/BST) |
| `America/New_York` | US Eastern (EST/EDT) |
| `America/Chicago` | US Central (CST/CDT) |
| `Asia/Tokyo` | Japan (JST) |

### Changing Email and Password

For security reasons, changing email or password requires confirmation of your **current password**.

1. Enter your **current password** in the dedicated field
2. Enter the new email or new password
3. Click **Save changes**

## Preferences

### Language

The application supports:
- 🇮🇹 **Italian**
- 🇬🇧 **English** (default)

The language is auto-detected from the browser. It can be changed in settings.

### Theme

You can switch between **dark** and **light** theme using the ☀️/🌙 button in the top bar.

The preference is saved in the browser.

---

## Security

- Sessions have a limited duration (JWT token with expiry)
- When the token expires you will be redirected to the login page
- Never share your credentials
- Use a strong password of at least 8 characters
